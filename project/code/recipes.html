Berry Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup frozen mango
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup raspberries
  • 1 cup milk or milk alternative
  • granola (optional)
Overview:

Smoothies are one of my favorite go-to breakfasts. They are very filling and a great way to incorporate fruit into your diet. I often find it difficult to buy fruit that isn’t frozen because it doesn’t stay fresh for a long time. You can make this smoothie with almost any combination of ingredients but I found the proportion of liquid to fruit to work very well for smoothie bowls.

Method
  1. Put ingredients in blender and mix at high speed until it reaches your desired consistency
  2. Either serve as a drink or in smoothie bowl and top it off with granola

Avocado Toast

Ingredients
  • Whole wheat sourdough bread
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 3 slices tomato
  • Arugula
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)
  • Hot sauce (optional)
Overview:

This breakfast is quick to make and can be adapted to any ingredients you have available at the time. All you really need in egg, avocado, and toast but this version of the recipe has become my personal favorite.

Method
  1. Toast slice of bread in toaster or toaster oven to desired amount
  2. Heat and oil a skillet on medium heat
  3. Fry egg until the edges are crispy and golden (around 4 minutes)
  4. Assemble toast: spread the mashed avocado, and add tomato slices, egg, and arugula on top
  5. Glaze with balsamic reduction or hot sauce

Banana Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Maple syrup (optional)
  • Berries (optional)
Overview:

Banana pancakes are such a simple and fun weekend option for when you’re craving something sweet but not too sweet. Regular pancakes are usually too sweet and bland for me but these banana pancakes always hit the spot.

Method
  1. Combine flour, white sugar, baking powder and salt
  2. In another bowl, mix together the egg, milk, vegetable oil and banana
  3. Stir the flour mixture into banana mixture
  4. Oil lightly and heat a frying pan over medium heat
  5. Pour ¼ cup of batter into pan at a time and cook until golden brown on both sides
  6. Serve with maple syrup and berries

Turkey Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of turkey
  • Pesto
  • 2 Slices of Whole Wheat Sourdough Bread
  • 1/2 Tomato
  • 1/4 cup of Arugula
  • 1/2 Avocado
  • 1-3 slices of cheese
Overview:

I recently discovered how good sandwiches are and I came across a similar recipe online and loved it. This is my absolute favorite sandwich. The pesto and avocado work together very nicely to create a filling and nutritious breakfast/lunch.

Method
  1. Toast the slices of whole wheat sourdough bread in a toaster or toaster oven to your liking
  2. Mash half of an avocado onto one piece of bread and spread pesto on the other slice
  3. Fill the sandwich with the remaining ingredients and enjoy

Vegan Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices Whole Wheat Sourdough Bread
  • 1/2 Avocado
  • 1/2 can of chickpeas
  • 1 tsp of olive oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
Overview:

This is another one of my favorite sandwiches and it's also a tasty vegan option. The chickpeas and avocado are a great source of nutrition and it’s also very quick to make and surprisingly filling.

Method
  1. Toast the slices of bread in a toaster or toaster oven to your desired amount
  2. Put the chickpeas into a medium sized bowl and mash them with the olive oil
  3. In a small bowl, mash the avocado and combine with lemon juice and pepper and salt to taste
  4. Spread the chickpeas on one side of the bread and the avocado on the other side

Chicken & Veggie Pasta

Ingredients

  • Trader Joe's pre-cooked rosemary seasoned chicken or 1 chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup of mushrooms
  • 1 Zucchini
  • 3/4 cup of pasta (Penne or Rigatoni recommended)
  • 2-3 tablespoons Olive Oil
  • Mushroom Umami seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parmesan (optional)
Overview:

This has been one of my favorite dinner options lately. Sometimes I’ll make it with brown rice instead of pasta for a more healthy alternative. I recommend using the Trader Joe’s Heat & Serve Balsamic Vinegar and Rosemary Chicken.

Method
  1. Boil water in a saucepan for the pasta
  2. Chop the mushrooms, zucchini, and chicken into medium sized pieces
  3. Put pasta in boiling water
  4. Heat and oil a skillet
  5. Cook the mushrooms, zucchini, and chicken in the skillet and season with Trader Joe's mushroom company umami seasoning or any other seasoning of your choice
  6. Drain pasta and add to skillet
  7. Combine all the ingredients and add parmesan to taste

Spicy Vodka Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1/4 cup of tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon vodka
  • 1/4 green onion
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 3/4 cup pasta (rigatoni or penne recommended)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • basil (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup parmesan
Overview:

This spicy penne alla vodka has been all over the internet lately but it is one of my personal favorite classics. It can be more spicy depending on the amount of pepper flakes you use. For a healthier option you can use whole wheat or lentil pasta.

Method
  1. Put the onion, garlic and olive oil in a large saucepan and cook until soft
  2. Add in the heavy cream and vodka and cook through until evaporated
  3. Stir until combined and season and remove sauce from the heat
  4. Cook pasta in a separate pan (save ¼ cup of the pasta water)
  5. Add butter to sauce and stir until it melts over medium heat
  6. Put cooked pasta and pasta water into saucepan
  7. Sprinkle basil and parmesan on top

Fajitas

Ingredients

  • Trader Joe’s Carne Asada
  • Mini Corn or flour tortillas
  • ½ Yellow onion
  • ½ Red pepper
  • ½ tablespoon Olive Oil
  • Sour cream (optional)
Overview:

These steak fajitas are super tasty and easy to make if you buy Trader Joe's carne asada. You can also customize them to your liking with whatever toppings and sides you like.

Method
  1. Heat medium skillet and cook the carne asada according to the directions on the package
  2. While the meat is cooking, slice up the vegetables into medium sized pieces
  3. Remove meat when it is done cooking and put it to the side
  4. Oil the same pan the meat was cooking in and cook the vegetables
  5. Place tortillas in the oven or toaster oven to warm up
  6. Assemble the fajitas and serve with sour cream
